About The Founder

A Problem Worth Solving

WHOLECAKE GROUP was founded on a simple but powerful observation: the modern workplace has evolved significantly in how it approaches employee wellbeing — yet one fundamental need for millions of female employees continues to be overlooked. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from HR managers and decision makers about the WHOLECAKE Wellness programme.

How does the monthly allocation work?
Each registered female employee receives a defined monthly product allocation. This allocation resets at the beginning of each month. Employees can only collect up to their allocated amount — preventing overconsumption while ensuring consistent access throughout the month.
Can monthly limits be customised for our organisation?
Yes. Allocation limits are fully customisable. During onboarding, we work with you to set allocation levels that make sense for your workforce size, budget and requirements. Limits can be adjusted over time as the programme matures.
How does replenishment work?
Stock levels are monitored continuously. WHOLECAKE manages the replenishment process proactively — when stock reaches a defined threshold, a replenishment order is initiated. Employers are notified and never left with an empty programme.
Is reporting included in the programme?
Yes. Reporting is a core feature of the WHOLECAKE programme. Employers receive regular reports covering usage summaries, allocation vs collection data, stock levels and programme trends. Reports can be used for ESG reporting, HR performance reviews and budget planning.
Can the programme scale across multiple sites?
Yes. The programme is designed to scale across multiple locations. Each site can have its own stock point, allocation settings and reporting. Central management retains visibility across all sites via consolidated reporting.
How are employees authenticated when accessing products?
The authentication process is discussed and confirmed during the consultation and onboarding phase, and is designed to be simple, private and easy to use for employees. Our goal is to ensure the process is dignified and does not create unnecessary friction for employees needing access.
Can usage limits be adjusted after the programme launches?
Yes. Usage limits can be reviewed and adjusted at any time. WHOLECAKE works collaboratively with each client — if reporting shows that current limits are too low or too high, we adjust accordingly to optimise the programme for your specific workforce.

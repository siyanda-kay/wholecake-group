About WHOLECAKE GROUP

Practical Business Solutions. Real Workplace Impact.

WHOLECAKE GROUP was established to address a practical, often overlooked need within South African workplaces — ensuring that female employees have consistent, dignified access to essential menstrual hygiene products. We believe that employee wellbeing extends far beyond remuneration and benefits packages.

When female employees have reliable access to essential hygiene products at work, it directly impacts their comfort, confidence and productivity — and by extension, the performance of the business. Our mission is to help organisations create workplaces where every employee feels supported.

WHOLECAKE GROUP exists to bridge this gap through practical, manageable solutions that integrate seamlessly into existing HR and facilities management frameworks. Our goal is to build long-term partnerships with organisations committed to meaningful, measurable employee wellbeing.
